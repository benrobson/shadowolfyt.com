Adding a Bot to your Guild


Have you created a bot application yet? If not here is a guide of how to. Click Here.

Obtain Client ID

Head over to the Discord Developer Application page. Find and select your bot that you would like to add to the Discord guild.

Set up Bot Permissions

Take the client ID that you have and take it over to this page. This allows you to give the bot the permissions you want it to have.

When you have finished selecting all permissions that you would like the bot to have put your client ID in the client id field.

Invite the bot to the guild

At the bottom of the page take the OAuth URL Generator link and copy and paste it into your Web Address bar.

It will display a screen and all you have to do is choose which guild you would like the bot to be in.

Once you've selected the guild hit the Authorize button.

Boom! You now have your own bot in your Discord guild!
